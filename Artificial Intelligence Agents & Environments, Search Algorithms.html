Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence Agents & Environments, Search Algorithms

During the lectures on artificial intelligence agents and environments, I learned about the different types of agents and their capabilities, such as reflex agents, model-based reflex agents, and goal-based agents. We also discussed various search algorithms, including uninformed search algorithms like breadth-first search and depth-first search, and informed search algorithms like A* search. Additionally, we learned about heuristic functions, which are used to estimate the cost of a solution and guide the search process towards more promising paths. The application of these concepts in developing intelligent agents capable of solving complex problems in real-world environments was also discussed.
