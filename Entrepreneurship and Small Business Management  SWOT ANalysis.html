Introduction to Business Management

Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management / SWOT ANalysis

In my entrepreneurship and small business management lectures, I learned about the importance of conducting a SWOT analysis for a business. A SWOT analysis involves assessing the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of a business, which can help identify areas for improvement and potential challenges. We discussed how a SWOT analysis can be used in the business planning process, as well as in ongoing management and decision-making. I also learned how to create an effective SWOT analysis by gathering relevant information, prioritizing key factors, and using the analysis to inform strategic decisions.
