Human Centred Computing

Requirement, Scenarios and Personas

During my lectures on teamwork and introduction to software project management, I learned about the importance of effective communication and collaboration in project teams. We were introduced to various tools and techniques for project management such as Agile and Waterfall methodologies, Gantt charts, and project timelines. We also discussed the roles and responsibilities of team members, and how to handle conflicts and challenges within a team. Additionally, we learned about the different stages of the software development life cycle, and how project management techniques can be used to ensure successful project completion. Overall, these lectures provided me with a strong foundation in teamwork and project management skills, which will be valuable in my future career as a software developer.
