Artificial Intelligence

Unsupervised Learning & Artificial Neural Network

During my lectures, I have learned about unsupervised learning and artificial neural networks (ANNs) in the context of machine learning. Unsupervised learning involves finding patterns and relationships in data without the use of labeled examples. This is in contrast to supervised learning, which requires labeled data for training. ANNs are a type of machine learning model that are loosely based on the structure and function of the human brain. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes that process information and learn to make predictions based on input data. ANNs can be used for a variety of tasks such as classification, regression, and clustering.
